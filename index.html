ABOUT ME

Who I am

I am Grace Amondi

A GIS developer based in Nairobi, Kenya

I design and develop web based Geospatial solutions ranging from basic web maps, to more sophisticated and advanced spatially-enabled web applications. My passion is to develop interactive, user-friendly, state of the art GIS tools, Web apps and systems that are able to foster the needs of my community.
